SPLASHTAILS

Location: Anywhere

Circumstances: None

Bait: Anything


Types of Splashtails:
- Ruby Spashtail
- Sunny Splashtail
- Indigo Splashtail

Can be found only on night:
- Seafoam Splashtail (glows)

Rarest one:
- Umbra Splashtail

: Splashtails are useful for the hunter events to obtain the cosmetics and rewards.

PONDIES

Location: Rivers, Ponds in the isles.

Circumstances: None

Bait: None


Types of Pondies:
- Charcoal Pondie
- Orchid Pondie
- Bronze Pondie

Can be found only on night:
- Moonsky Pondie (glows)

Rarest one:
- Bright Pondie

: to improve Pondies grinding, only one player should cast the hook because of the smal size of their locations.

8

 

BATTLEGILLS

Location: Everywhere

Circumstances: Near active forts or skeleton ships

Bait: Grubs


Types of Battlegills:
- Jade Battlegill
- Sky Battlegill
- Rum Battlegill

Can be found only on night:
- Bittersweet Battlegill (glows)

Rarest one:
- Sand Battlegill

: when trying to catch the Battlegills you may prefer fishing during a fort rather than a skeleton ship because they could sink yours.


: Sand Battlegill is one of the rarest fish in the game. It is worth over 14k gold!

9

 

STORMFISHES

Location: Everywhere

Circumstances: Into storms

Bait: Leeches


Types of Stormfishes:
- Ancient Stormfish
- Shores Stormfish
- Wilds Stormfish

Can be found only on night:
- Twilight Stormfish (glows)

Rarest one:
- Shadow Stormfish

: you could be struck by a lightning while fishing into thunderstorms, being full health and fishing into the water may prevent from catching on fire or die.

10

 

WRECKERS

Blackclouds

Location: Near shipwrecks

Circumstances: None, storm for the Blackclouds

Bait: Earthworms


Types of Wreckers:
- Rose Wrecker
- Sun Wreckers
- Blackcloud Wreckers (tremendously rare)

Can be found only on night:
- Moon Wreckers (glows)

Rarest one:
- Snow Wreckers

: prepare yourself with a lot of time and patience, Wreckers are the rarest fish in th e game.
