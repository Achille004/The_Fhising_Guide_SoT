01

SPLASHTAILS

Location: Anywhere

Circumstances: None

Bait: Anything

Common:

  • Ruby Spashtail
  • Sunny Splashtail
  • Indigo Splashtail

Only at night (glows):

  • Seafoam Splashtail

Rarest one:

  • Umbra Splashtail
: Splashtails are useful for the hunter events to obtain the cosmetics and rewards.

02

PLENTIFINS

Location: Spoils of Plenty

Circumstances: None

Bait: Earthworms

Common:

  • Olive Plentifin
  • Amber Plentifin
  • Cloudy Plentifin

Only at night (glows):

  • Watery Plentifin

Rarest one:

  • Bonedust Plentifin

03

ANCIENTSCALES

Location: Ancient Isles

Circumstances: None

Bait: Leeches

Common:

  • Almond Ancientscale
  • Sapphire Ancientscale
  • Smoke Ancientscale

Only at night (glows):

  • Starshine Ancientscale

Rarest one:

  • Bone Ancientscale
: Bone Ancientscale is hard to find, if you manage to catch one you are very lucky man.

04

WILDSPLASHES

Location: Wilds

Circumstances: None

Bait: Earthworms

Common:

  • Russet Wildsplash
  • Sandy Wildsplash
  • Ocean Wildsplash

Only at night (glows):

  • Coral Wildsplash

Rarest one:

  • Muddy Wildsplash

05

DEVILFISHES

Location: Devil's Roar

Circumstances: None

Bait: Grubs

Common:

  • Ashen Devilfish
  • Seashell Devilfish
  • Lava Devilfish

Only at night (glows):

  • Firelight Devilfish

Rarest one:

  • Forsaken Devilfish (pretty rare)
: in the Devil's Roar, fishing near volcanoes can be very dangerous for you, especially when grinding the Forsaken Devilfish. Position yourself wisely.

06

ISLEHOOPERS

Location: Everywhere

Circumstances: Near isles (possibly larger ones)

Bait: None

Common:

  • Stone Islehooper
  • Moss Islehooper
  • Honey Islehooper

Only at night (glows):

  • Amethyst Islehooper

Rarest one:

  • Raven Islehooper

07

PONDIES

Location: Rivers, Ponds in the isles.

Circumstances: None

Bait: None

Common:

  • Charcoal Pondie
  • Orchid Pondie
  • Bronze Pondie

Only at night (glows):

  • Moonsky Pondie

Rarest one:

  • Bright Pondie
: to improve Pondies grinding, only one player should cast the hook because of the small size of their locations.

08

BATTLEGILLS

Location: Everywhere

Circumstances: Near active forts or skeleton ships

Bait: Grubs

Common:

  • Jade Battlegill
  • Sky Battlegill
  • Rum Battlegill

Only at night (glows):

  • Bittersweet Battlegill

Rarest one:

  • Sand Battlegill
: when trying to catch the Battlegills you may prefer fishing during a fort rather than a skeleton ship because they could sink yours.

: Sand Battlegill is one of the rarest fish in the game. It is worth over 14k gold!

09

STORMFISHES

Location: Everywhere

Circumstances: Into storms

Bait: Leeches

Common:

  • Ancient Stormfish
  • Shores Stormfish
  • Wilds Stormfish

Only at night (glows):

  • Twilight Stormfish

Rarest one:

  • Shadow Stormfish
: you could be struck by a lightning while fishing into thunderstorms, being full health and fishing into the water may prevent from catching on fire or die.

10

WRECKERS

Blackclouds

Location: Near shipwrecks

Circumstances: None, storm for the Blackclouds

Bait: Earthworms

Common:

  • Rose Wrecker
  • Sun Wreckers
  • Blackcloud Wreckers (tremendously rare)

Only at night (glows):

  • Moon Wreckers

Rarest one:

  • Snow Wreckers
: prepare yourself with a lot of time and patience, Wreckers are the rarest fish in the game.
