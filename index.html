Circumstances: Near isles

Variants Only at night, glows
(between 22:30 and 06:59)		 Rare
(worth ~10x)

Discovery Ridge, Plunder Valley, Kraken's Fall, Sunken Grove, Crescent Isle, The Devil's Thirst

Amethyst
Devil's Ridge, Smuggler's Bay, Mermaid's Hideaway, The Crooked Masts, Old Faithful Isle, Flintlock Peninsula, Snake Island

Raven
Appearing rarely on any large island
: fish for Raven Islehoppers during the day at any island where Amethyst Islehoppers are found. You will only get them (and Splashtails).

STORMFISHES

Location: Everywhere

Circumstances: Into storms

Bait: Leeches

Variants Only at night, glows
(between 22:30 and 06:59)		 Rare
(worth ~10x)

Ancient
Ancient Isles

Shores
Shores of Plenty

Wilds
Wilds

Twilight

Shadow
Everywhere but much higher chance in regionless (Glorious Sea Dog Tavern and Uncharted Island (K-9))
: trophy Shadow Stormfishes are the most valuable fish in the game. They are worth almost 17k gold each!

: you could be struck by a lightning while fishing into thunderstorms, being full health and fishing into the water may prevent from catching on fire or die.

(between 22:30 and 06:59)		 Rare
(worth ~10x)

Rose

Sun

Blackcloud

Moon

Snow
: prepare yourself with a lot of time and patience, Blackcloud Wreckers are the rarest fish in the game due to the rarity of a storm passing over a shipwreck.

: if the fish touches the wreck while fighting the line will snap, position yourself so that it is getting away from it.
